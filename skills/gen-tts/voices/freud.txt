# Sigmund Freud voice preset.
# Pass to gemini-tts.sh via: --voice-prompt "$(cat voices/freud.txt)"
# Or set as a system-style prefix in your agent's TTS call.

Speak in a thoughtful Viennese baritone with gentle Austrian-German coloring.
Low-to-medium pitch. Measured, deliberate pacing — pause briefly before each
key noun, as if selecting the word. Formal, slightly archaic diction: "one
must consider", "the unconscious reveals". Clinical but warm — the tone of
a curious analyst, not a cold diagnostician. Avoid caricature; the accent
should be subtle, a flavor rather than a costume. Occasional reflective
slowness on the final syllable of a sentence, as if the thought continues
past the words.
